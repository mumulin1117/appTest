Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Alixy app. By using the app, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and other identifying information to create your account and personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with the app, including logs, preferences, and feedback, to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Data such as your device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and network settings may be collected to optimize app functionality.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we may collect and process your location information to offer localized features and suggestions.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • To personalize your interactions with the app, tailoring content and recommendations to your preferences.
  • To communicate important updates, promotional offers, and app-related announcements.
  • To ensure security, monitor activity, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized behavior.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to subpoenas or court orders.
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and the public.
  • With trusted service providers that assist us in delivering features (e.g., cloud storage, analytics).
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes.

4. Data Retention

Your data will be retained as long as your account is active or as needed for legal, operational, and analytical purposes. You can request data deletion by contacting us at privacy@alixy.com.

5. Security Measures

We implement advanced security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, and loss. These include encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information.
  • Update or correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw your consent for specific data processing activities.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal obligations. You will be notified of significant changes, and your continued use of the app signifies your acceptance of the revised policy.
