Poos Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you use Poos, we may collect:

  • Photography Data: Pose preferences, editing patterns, and AI suggestion adoption rates
  • Device Information: Camera specifications, lighting conditions, and EXIF metadata
  • Usage Analytics: Feature interaction data and community engagement metrics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Improve AI-powered pose recommendations
  • Enhance community features and content moderation
  • Develop new photography tools and filters
  • Ensure platform security and prevent misuse

3. Data Protection

We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for photo processing
  • Automatic EXIF data removal for shared content
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing

4. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Delete account-associated content
  • Opt-out of AI training datasets
  • Export creative pose collections
